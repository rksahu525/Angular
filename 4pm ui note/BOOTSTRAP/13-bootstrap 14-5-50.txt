Responsive Navigation Bar
- Navigation is designed by using <nav> element.
- It must have the role  as "navigation"
- The classes are

.navbar
.navbar-expand- {sm, md, lg, xl}
.navbar-brand
.navbar-nav
.navbar-toggler
.navbar-toggler-icon
.navbar-text
.collapse.navbar-collapse
.nav-item
.nav-link
.fixed-top
.fixed-bottom
.sticky-top
